Plants

Photosynthesis

Process by which plants and some other organisms use light energy to covert water and carbon dioxide into glucose(sugars) and oxygen

Chlorophyll

Green pigment in plants that absorbs light energy used to carry out photosynthesis

Respiration

The process by which cells obtain energy from glucose

More Processes

Transpiration

The process by which plants release water vapor into the air through stomata(pores)

Asexual Plant Reproduction

When an entire plant grows from one leaf or just a portion of the stem or root

Dormancy

When the organism's growth stops

More facts

Phototropism

Growth of a plant away from light

Gravitropism

A change in the growth of a plant in response to gravity

Chloroplast

Organelle that captures the energy from sunlight and converts it into chemical energy
