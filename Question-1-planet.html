Planets

Mercury

Size

The radius of Mercury is 1516 miles.

Weather

Mercury typically doesn't contain a typical weather. The temperature can raise up to 801°F in the day or be as low as -279°F in the night.

Fun Fact

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and completes a single year in only 88 Earth days.

Venus

Size

Venus is much larger than Mercury and contains a radius of 3760.4 miles compared to Mercury's radius for 1516 miles.

Weather

There are thick clouds of sulfuric acid trapping heat in, thus the temperatures can reach over 800°F.

Fun Fact

The atmosphere of Venus has a pressure of 90 times higher than Earth.

Earth

Size

The Earth is larger than Venus and has a radius of 3963 miles.

Weather

The global average temperature of Earth is 58.3°F and has four seasons due to its 23.5° tilt.

Fun Fact

The Earth is the only planet known to have life on it.

Mars

Size

Mars has a radius smaller than Earth at 2,106 miles.

Weather

The planet averages around -80°F because of its thin atmosphere which is unable to trap heat.

Fun Fact

The gravity of Mars is 37% of what we have on Earth.

Jupiter

Size

Jupiter has a radius of 43,441 miles, the largest planet in our solar system.

Weather

Jupiter has temperatures ranging from -190°F to 40°F. The planet has constant hurricane-like storms.

Fun Fact

In Jupiter’s red spot, the winds can reach around 384 miles per hour.

Saturn

Size

Saturn has a radius of 36,184 miles.

Weather

Saturn has an average temperature of -288°F.

Fun Fact

Saturn has 62 moons unlike Earth’s one moon.

Uranus

Size

Uranus has a radius of 15,759 miles.

Weather

Uranus is said to have a weather of -300°F.

Fun Fact

The gravity’s force is about 86% of the strength of Earth’s.

Neptune

Size

The radius of the planet is 15,299 miles.

Weather

Neptune has a temperature of -353°F.

Fun Fact

Neptune has no definite surface and is said to be made up of slushy ice and water.

Pluto

Size

Pluto has a radius of 738 miles. The United States is about two times as wide as Pluto is.

Weather

The weather ranges from -369°F to -385F°.

Fun Fact

Many scientists argue that Pluto is no longer considered a planet due to its small size compared to other planets and asteroids.
