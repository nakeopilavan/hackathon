Introduction to Science

We hope to introduce youths to new and exciting scientific concepts, in hopes of sparking a flame of interest. Moreover, we hope to push this interest through the students academic year, helping them grow and become more assertive. The exicitement of unknown, struggle of understanding, and content from discovery will help develope a growth mindset, to continue struggling and learning despite failure.
